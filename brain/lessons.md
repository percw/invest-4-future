# Lessons — what worked, what didn't, and why

Append-only post-mortems. Format: dated entry, what happened, what to
encode in the playbook / mandate as a result.

## 2025-09-10 — ASML position: thesis intact through drawdown

EUV monopoly thesis held even as the stock corrected on China-export
noise. Did not trim. Lesson: **mechanism-driven theses tolerate price
volatility; only falsification triggers warrant action.**

→ Encoded in `mandate.md`: "Drawdown is not a signal."

## 2024-08-20 — NVDA: bought near top of cycle

Entered at 450 USD on AI-infra thesis. Stock fell to ~200. Thesis was
generic ("AI infra is the future") rather than mechanism-specific.
Pure-play % to any one thesis was already < 50 % at scale.

Lesson: **at scale, broad winners stop being pure-plays.** The 50 %
pure-play rule existed for this reason — applied it inconsistently.

→ Encoded: pure-play test must be re-applied annually for held
positions, not just at entry.
