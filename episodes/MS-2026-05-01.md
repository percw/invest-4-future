# EP #252 — Google Invests $40B Into Anthropic, GPT 5.5 Drops, and Google Cloud Dominates

## Raw notes / transcript snippets

- 15 major AI releases in 8 weeks
- Moonshot AI launches Kimi K2.6 (Diamandis ran it on Mac Studio,
  orchestrated by Opus 4.6)
- OpenAI ships GPT-5.5
- DeepSeek 4 released
- Google: $40B commitment to Anthropic ($10B initial at $350B valuation,
  +$30B as milestones met). Google Cloud provides 5 GW capacity over
  5 years; Anthropic anchor expands to 3.5 GW on TPU in 2027.
- Google unveils 8th-gen TPUs: **TPU 8T (training)**, **TPU 8I
  (inference)**. Claims +2.8x training price-perf, +80 % inference
  price-perf vs Ironwood.
- Google projects 4.3M TPU shipments 2026 → 35M by 2028.
- Wissner-Gross: **"the actual bottleneck to all AI is at TSM"** —
  TSMC advanced packaging.
- Anthropic revenue trajectory: $1B (end-2024) → $9B (end-2025) →
  $30B (early April 2026). Annualized.
- Amazon and Anthropic trading cash for compute (separate from Google deal).
