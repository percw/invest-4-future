## Claim

TSMC advanced packaging (CoWoS-L) remains the binding constraint on AI
compute supply through 2026 H2 and into 2027, supporting structural
pricing power and gross-margin uplift toward 60 %+.

## Mechanism

All AI accelerator roadmaps (NVIDIA Rubin, Google TPU 8T/8I, AMD MI400,
Meta / OpenAI / Anthropic custom silicon) route through CoWoS-L. TSMC
expansion from 35k to 130k WPM by end-2026 still trails compounding
demand — Anthropic alone commits to 5 GW of compute via Google. Mix
shifts to higher-ASP CoWoS-L; gross margin uplift follows.

## Consensus view

TSMC = cyclical foundry leader, ~25x forward PE. Sell-side capacity
models suggest CoWoS bottleneck eases in H2 2026.

## Variant perception

Wissner-Gross (EP #252): "the actual bottleneck to all AI is at TSM."
Bottleneck persists into 2027 due to compounding TPU + Rubin + custom
silicon demand. TSMC behaves more like an AI infrastructure royalty
than a cyclical foundry — deserves structural multiple re-rating.
