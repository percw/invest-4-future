## Claim

Hybrid bonding (HB) equipment sells out into 2027 as HBM4 mass production
ramps, driving a leveraged equipment-cycle for the dominant supplier.

## Mechanism

HBM4 stacks (12-hi and beyond) require hybrid bonding for thermal /
density reasons. SK Hynix and Micron HBM4 ramp drives 2-3x HB tool
orders in 2026-2027. BESI is sole-source on production HB lines
(~70 % market share). Anthropic's 30x revenue growth + 5 GW compute
commit + TPU 8T / Rubin Ultra HBM4 demand all converge on HB capacity.

## Consensus view

BESI = cyclical Dutch semi-cap. HBM4 pushed to late 2026 / 2027.
Stock has been weak on Samsung HBM4 stumbles. Sell-side EPS for 2027
implicitly assumes HB ramp trails into late 2027.

BESI gets pricing power as the only at-scale HB tool supplier.
