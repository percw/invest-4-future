## Claim

Google TPU 8I captures 20-30 % of inference accelerator market by 2028,
breaking the assumption that NVIDIA holds 80 %+ share indefinitely.

## Mechanism

TPU 8I delivers +80 % inference price-performance vs Ironwood. Anthropic
anchor expands to 3.5 GW on TPU in 2027. Google projects 4.3M TPU
shipments 2026 → 35M by 2028. Agentic workloads multiply inference per
query 10-100x, favoring specialized inference silicon.

## Consensus view

NVIDIA holds 80 %+ accelerator share indefinitely. TPU is captive Google
workload only.

## Variant perception

TPU 8I is structurally cheaper at hyperscale inference. Anthropic anchor
opens enterprise inference share via GCP. Bifurcated training / inference
market emerges.

## Why rejected (verdict: priced-in / not investable)

- No pure-play public expression. GOOGL TPU + cloud is < 25 % of
  consolidated revenue → fails 50 % pure-play screen.
- AVGO and MRVL custom-silicon AI revenue 25-30 % → also below threshold.
- Best indirect expression is via H1 (TSM) — all custom AI silicon
  routes through TSMC anyway, so the bull case for TPU is already a
  bull case for TSM.
