Sole at-scale supplier of CoWoS-L advanced packaging — toll-bridge for
every AI accelerator regardless of architecture (NVIDIA, TPU, custom).

## Why this instrument

NYSE ADR is the cleanest single-stock expression of H1. Wissner-Gross
variant: TSMC is the *actual* bottleneck. ADR is not ASK-eligible, but
no equivalent EU listing — TPE common stock not accessible from
Norwegian retail brokers.

Alternative considered: ASML (already held) — EUV, supplier to TSMC.
Complementary but different mechanism (lithography monopoly vs
packaging monopoly).
